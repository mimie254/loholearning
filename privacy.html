Privacy Policy

LoHo Learning ("we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our platform.

Legal framework

We comply with the Kenya Data Protection Act (2019) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We process your data lawfully and transparently.

Data we collect

We may collect information you provide when registering (e.g. name, email, school or institution), usage data (e.g. progress, device information), and technical data such as cookies and similar technologies to improve the service and analyse usage.

How we use your data

We use your data to provide and improve the platform, communicate with you, comply with legal obligations, and ensure security. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience, analyse usage, and deliver personalised content. You can manage cookie settings in your browser.

Your rights

You have the right to access, rectify, delete, or object to the processing of your personal data. You may request a copy of your data (data portability) or account deletion by contacting us at support@loholearning.co.ke. We will respond to deletion requests within 30 days and retain data only where required by law.

Children and minors

LoHo Learning is designed for K–12 learners. We take extra care with minors' data and require verifiable parental or guardian consent before a child may use the platform. See our Terms & Conditions for details.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email, in-app notification, or a notice on our site. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

For questions or requests about your personal data or this policy, contact us at support@loholearning.co.ke.

About LoHo Learning

LoHo Learning provides affordable, accessible, and curriculum-aligned digital learning for every child.
